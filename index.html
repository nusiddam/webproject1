Types of Motorcycles

Sport

Sport bikes are designed for speed. They have powerful engines and lightweight frames. Some popular sport bike brands include Yamaha, Kawasaki, and BMW. These motorcycles are primarily made for racing.

Crusier

Crusier bikes are known as the standard motorcycle.They have a lower seat and more relaxed riding postion. Some popular crusier bikes include Harley-Davidson, Honda, and Suzuki. These motorcycles are primarily made for street riding.
